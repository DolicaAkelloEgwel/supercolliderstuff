## Digital Music

### MIDI

Lowest note is C-1 with a MIDI number of 0. Each octave has 12 numbers. Each _half-step_ or _semitone_ is a number.

Note | MIDI Number
--- | ---
C0 | 12
C1 | 24
C2 | 32
C3 | 48
C4 | 60
